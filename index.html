Researcher working at the intersection of machine learning, neural signal processing, healthcare data science, and human-centered robotics.

I am currently a Research Assistant at Dignity Health / Barrow Neurological Institute, where I build end-to-end pipelines for patient data and analyze intracranial EEG from epilepsy patients during emotional memory and cognitive tasks. My broader work spans AI for clinical decision support, social touch sensing for assistive robots, and EMG-to-speech interfaces.
